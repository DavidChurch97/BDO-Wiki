Hunting

Information How to get a hunting rifle How to hunt Hunting exp quests

Information

You can begin solo hunting at level 30+ if you have a hunting rifle. To hunt harder monsters such as the Blue Whale, you will need a high hunting level and at least one full party of players. Hunting can be profitable once you reach higher levels but it is very time consuming. Some cooking recipes also use whale meat which can only be collect by hunting.

How to get a hunting rifle

To get a hunting rifle, you can either buy it from the item market under the “primary weapon” tab, or you have to craft it yourself using a tool workshop

If you are just starting out you will need to use the “Hunting for Beginners Hand Canvas” which requires Copper Ore x4 and Blackstone Powder x1. It will take approximately 20 minutes (worker speed 50).

Hunting rifles can be repaired by the blacksmith just like all other weapons.

How to hunt

Once you have your hunting rifle and Tanning Knife/Butcher Knife, go to one of the locations above to search for your prey.

Equip your gun by right-clicking it then aim the cross-hair at the whale/crocodile. If the cross-hair is red you will have a chance of hitting and damaging the monster. It can take several minutes to kill them but you can crouch by pressing the SPACE key to higher your range. You can run and roll with the gun equipped but you can’t interact with objects so you will have to take the weapon off to gather from the corpse. I recommend putting the gun and your main hand on your hot bar so you can switch easily from one to the other.

When the whale/crocodile/Boar/Deer is killed, stand on top of them to receive the loot. Make sure you have your Tanning Knife/Butcher Knife equipped then press “R” to collect. A Tanning Knife will give you hide and a Butcher Knife will give you meat. Hunting in parties will not share EXP because you receive the hunting EXP when you loot the prey and only one person can loot.

Non-repeatabale quests

Hunting in the Forest of Seclusion

  • Requirements: 40 Amity with Hessenvale
  • Start NPC: Hessenvale (Toscani Farm, Balenos)
  • Objective: Get 1 quest item – Owl, Drop chance: 100%
  • Rewards: Contribution EXP 50, Recover 2 Energy, Hunting Skill Experience
  • This quest also requires you to use a Practise Matchlock, which can be rented from Ernill in Velia or Hessenvale at the Western Guard Camp

Bee Hurts Farm

  • Requirements: Finished quest – Basics of Hunting
  • Start NPC: Daphne DelLucci (Marino Farm, Balenos)
  • Objective: Get 3 quest items – Wooden Beehive, Drop chance: 100%
  • Rewards: Contribution EXP 50, Recover 2 Energy, Hunting Skill Experience
  • This quest also requires you to use a Practise Matchlock, which can be rented from Daphne DelLucci

Shoot and Help

  • Requirements: Level > 29, Exploration (5) > 1
  • Start NPC: Daphne DelLucci (Marino Farm, Balenos)
  • Objective: Kill monsters (5) – Wooden Beehive
  • Rewards: Contribution EXP 60, [Hunting] Beginner Matchlock, Hunting Skill Experience
  • This quest also requires you to use a Practise Matchlock, which can be rented from Daphne DelLucci

Hunter-Loving Hunter

  • Requirements: Finished quest – Shoot and Help

Annoying Jellyfish
